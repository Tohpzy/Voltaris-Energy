FAQs

Common questions, answered.

How long does solar installation take?+
Most residential systems are installed within 3–7 days depending on size and site readiness. Commercial and industrial systems are scoped individually.
Do you offer maintenance after installation?+
Yes. Every installation comes with a maintenance plan option, plus a workmanship warranty and rapid-response support.
Can solar power run my whole building?+
In most cases, yes — we design hybrid systems that combine solar, battery storage and grid or generator backup based on your energy audit.
Do you serve locations outside Lagos?+
Yes, we've delivered projects across multiple Nigerian states for residential, commercial and industrial clients.
